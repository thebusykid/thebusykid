What are some age-appropriate activities for a busy book for kids?

Here are some age-appropriate activities that can be included in a busy book for kids:

  • For infants and toddlers (0-2 years old): simple board book pages with bright colors, patterns, and high-contrast images to help with visual development.
  • For 2-3 years old: simple matching games, button-and-loop closures, and zippers to help with fine motor development.
  • For 3-4 years old: simple puzzles, mazes, and tracing activities to help with problem-solving and pre-writing skills.
  • For 4-5 years old: simple counting and matching activities, dot-to-dot puzzles, and simple coloring pages to help with number recognition and early math skills.
  • For 5-6 years old: simple word matching and word building activities, simple mazes and hidden pictures to help with reading and writing readiness.

It's important to keep in mind that children develop at different rates, so the age ranges are just a general guide.
